New COVID-19 measures in effect as of December 20 2021

  • Passport vaccine will now be required to enter the church
  • All worshippers must remain seated during liturgy
  • It is obligatory to wear masks during liturgy
We are permitted to have a maximum of 89 persons in church during Liturgy. Going over this limit puts us at risk of incurring a fine of 6 000$ per person. For this reason after we have reached this number our front door will be locked.
