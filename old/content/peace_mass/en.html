Prayer for peace in Ukraine

On January 26, 2022, the whole world will unite in prayer for peace in Ukraine and the world, responding to the call of Pope Francis. The full-day prayer stand of the global community of the Ukrainian Greek Catholic Church will be held this day in a telemarathon on YouTube https://youtu.be/J9KE54tOGrQ. It will last from 9:00 to 21:00 Kiev time. (2:00AM - 2:00PM Eastern Standard Time).
