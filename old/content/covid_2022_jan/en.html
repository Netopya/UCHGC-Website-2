Dear Parishioners!

As of Monday, February 7, 2022, the Quebec Provincial Government is allowing churches to be opened. Beginning on February 13th we will resume celebrating Liturgy according to our regular schedule. During the month of February, Liturgy will begin at 11:00 AM.

For participation in the Liturgy the faithful must have a vaccination passport. Attendance is limited to 50% of capacity.

A vaccination passport is not required for participation in funerals. Attendance is limited to 50 persons.
